07. Token Tax Estimator

Description: Estimate your capital gains tax based on your buy/sell price, quantity, and country-specific tax rates.

Utility: Helps investors understand tax liabilities before realizing crypto gains.

How to Use: Enter the buy price, sell price, quantity of tokens, and select the tax rate. Then click "Estimate Tax".

Result Analysis: Shows your total gain and estimated tax owed, helping you budget for tax season or plan exits wisely.
