21. BTC/ETH Dominance Tracker

Description: This tool tracks the current dominance of Bitcoin and Ethereum in the overall crypto market.

Utility: Helps identify market trend shifts and investor focus based on BTC and ETH dominance data.

How to Use: Click the button to check the current dominance percentages of BTC and ETH.

Result Analysis: Higher BTC dominance indicates a risk-off environment. Rising ETH dominance may signal growing altcoin market activity.

22. NFT Floor Price Viewer

Description: Simulates the current floor prices of top NFT collections.

Utility: Allows NFT traders and collectors to visualize price trends and identify potential entry points.

How to Use: Click the button to simulate fetching floor price data for well-known collections.

Result Analysis: Useful for tracking value trends in NFT markets. Floor movements can indicate upcoming hype or corrections.
