35. Network Latency Checker

Description: This tool simulates a latency check to measure the response time between different blockchain networks.

Utility: Helps developers and traders identify fast or slow-performing networks for app deployment or transaction execution.

How to Use: Choose a network from the list and click "Check Latency" to view simulated results.

Result Analysis: Networks with lower latency are ideal for fast trading, DeFi interactions, or real-time apps. High latency may indicate congestion or inefficient routing.
