54. Meme Coin Detector

Description: Analyze token names and tickers to detect if they are likely meme coins based on keywords and patterns.

Utility: Avoid scams or hype-driven coins by spotting meme indicators quickly.

How to Use: Enter the token name or ticker and click "Detect".

Result Analysis: The result highlights red flags that may indicate a meme coin like excessive emojis, community slang, or unrealistic promises.
