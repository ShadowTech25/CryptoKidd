40. Token Volatility Scanner

Description: Analyze the 7-day volatility of selected tokens based on simulated price movements.

Utility: Helps traders gauge token price instability and prepare appropriate risk management strategies.

How to Use: Select a token from the dropdown and click "Scan Volatility" to view simulated percentage changes.

Result Analysis: High volatility can signal speculative trading or impending news events. Use this insight to adjust trade sizing and stop-loss placement.
