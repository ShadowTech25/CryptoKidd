16. Altcoin Season Index

Description: Calculates whether it’s currently Bitcoin season or Altcoin season based on market index simulation.

Utility: Helps with macro positioning—whether to lean heavier on BTC or diversify into altcoins.

How to Use: Click “Check” to simulate and determine the current index category.

Result Analysis: If the index shows Altcoin Season, smaller-cap coins might outperform. BTC Season may signal dominance safety plays.
