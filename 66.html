66. Web3 Identity Verifier

Description: Checks and verifies decentralized identity (DID) associated with an ENS or wallet address.

Utility: Useful for verifying the authenticity of decentralized identities in DAO applications, NFT provenance, and on-chain reputation.

How to Use: Enter a wallet or ENS name and click verify.

Result Analysis: Verified IDs confirm authenticity for trustless interactions. No record indicates a new or unauthenticated identity.
