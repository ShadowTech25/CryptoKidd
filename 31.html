31. Token Unlock Heatmap

Description: Visualize upcoming token unlock events and analyze their potential impact on market liquidity and price volatility.

Utility: Helps investors avoid sudden dumps and front-run unlock-related volatility by planning trades around scheduled token unlocks.

How to Use: Click the button to load a simulated unlock schedule including date, token, amount, and risk level.

Result Analysis: High-risk unlocks may signal incoming sell pressure. Plan entries/exits accordingly and monitor liquidity and volume.
